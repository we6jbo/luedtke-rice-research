Main Research Summary

The main line on this page follows Charles McQuiston, born 25 March 1850 in Butler County, Pennsylvania, and died 31 July 1917 in Butler, Butler County, Pennsylvania. His parents are listed as Charles McQuiston and Rebecca Grannis. The older McQuiston line points back to Robert A. McQuiston, born in Tobermore, Londonderry, Northern Ireland, and later connected to Slippery Rock, Butler County, Pennsylvania.

A key older maternal connection in the line is Margaret McKissock, born 27 February 1731 in Belfast, Antrim, Ireland, and died 30 May 1818 in North Sewickley, Beaver County, Pennsylvania. This page also preserves the Stitt-side connection through Margaret “Maggie” Louella Stitt McQuiston, Alexander D. Galbraith Stitt, John Stitt, and Margaret Ellen Appleby.

Research Caution

This page is a research-organizing page. It is designed to preserve names, dates, places, source links, and research leads. Each relationship should be verified against original or near-original records where possible, including census records, marriage records, probate files, land records, church records, published family histories, and local Pennsylvania, Ireland, and Scotland sources.

The McQuiston family book is an important source, but family-history books should still be compared with independent records. This page is meant to help researchers find the path, not to replace careful proof work.
