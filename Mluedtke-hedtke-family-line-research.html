Research Summary

This page tracks a German-Prussian and Pomeranian family research path involving the Hedtke and Luedtke families. The Hedtke line includes records for August Fredrich Hedtke, Johann E. Hedtke, Martin Hedtke, and Michael Hedtke. The Luedtke line includes Amelia Emilie Caroline Luedtke, Martin Luedtke, Johan Martin Luedke, and Johann Christian Lüdke.

The strongest working pattern is a migration and record trail from West Prussia / Pomerania into the United States, especially Minnesota and Wisconsin. The source set includes U.S. federal census records, Minnesota territorial and state census records, Lutheran baptism/marriage/burial records, passenger and immigration records, land ownership maps, and Find a Grave memorials.

Research caution: This page preserves the notes as research clues. Some dates conflict in the notes and should be verified before being treated as final. The most important conflicts are the death year for Johann E. Hedtke and the death dates listed for Michael Hedtke.
