Luedtke and Rice Research Project

Welcome to the public research page for the Luedtke and Rice family lines.

This page will collect evidence notes, surname variants, migration clues, and public discoveries.

Research surnames include: Luedtke, Luedke, Liedtke, Ludtke, and Rice.

Current focus

Current research focuses on connecting documented family lines, comparing surname variants, reviewing migration clues, and identifying record targets that may strengthen or disprove proposed links.
