Luedtke and Rice Research Project

Welcome to the public research page for the Luedtke and Rice family lines.

This page collects evidence notes, surname variants, migration clues, place names, and public discoveries.

Research surnames include: Luedtke, Luedke, Liedtke, Ludtke, Lüdtke, Rice, Riewe, Hartfiel, and Heymann.

Current focus

Current research focuses on connecting documented family lines, comparing surname variants, reviewing migration clues, and identifying record targets that may strengthen family connections.

Known surname variants

Luedtke, Luedke, Liedtke, Ludtke, Lüdtke, Rice, and Riewe.

Key places in this research

Grabowo, Krojanke, Flatow, West Prussia, Pomerania, Wisconsin, and Minnesota.

How to help

If you are connected to these family lines and recognize a surname, place, or family connection, please share your clue, record lead, or correction.

Status
